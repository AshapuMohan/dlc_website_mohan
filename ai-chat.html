Frequently Asked Questions

1. Open WhatsApp
2. Tap the chat icon (bottom right on Android, top right on iPhone)
3. Select a contact
4. Type your message in the text box
5. Tap the send button (paper plane icon)

1. Open Paytm app
2. Tap "Pay"
3. Enter the mobile number or scan QR code
4. Enter the amount
5. Tap "Pay" and enter your UPI PIN

1. Open Google Maps
2. Tap the directions button (blue circle with arrow)
3. Enter your starting location or use "Current Location"
4. Enter your destination
5. Choose your transportation method (car, public transit, walking)
6. Tap "Start" to begin navigation
